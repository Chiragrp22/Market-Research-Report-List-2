<p><p>Wafer analyzers refer to the instruments and systems used in semiconductor industries for analyzing the properties and characteristics of silicon wafers. De-embedding market, on the other hand, involves removing or subtracting the unwanted or interfering effects of probes, mounts, and fixtures from the measured data to accurately analyze the intrinsic properties of the wafer. Both these types of wafer analyzers play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and performance of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
