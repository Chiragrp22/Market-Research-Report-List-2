MAP involves altering the atmosphere inside the packaging to increase the product's shelf life. VSP utilizes a thin plastic film that tightly adheres to the poultry, providing an attractive presentation and extending shelf life. VTP involves heating and shaping plastic sheets to create customized packaging for poultry products. Other packaging types may include innovative technologies like active packaging, biodegradable materials, or smart packaging solutions to enhance freshness and safety of poultry products.</p></p>
