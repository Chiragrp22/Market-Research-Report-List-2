<p><strong>The Antigen Microarray Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autoantibody Detection</li><li>Biomarker Profiling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antigen microarray market consists of two types: autoantibody detection and biomarker profiling. In autoantibody detection, the arrays are designed to identify and measure antibodies produced by the immune system against an individual's own proteins. This helps in diagnosing autoimmune diseases. On the other hand, biomarker profiling involves the identification and quantification of specific proteins or other molecules present in bodily fluids. This helps in studying diseases, predicting patient outcome, and developing personalized treatments. Both types of antigen microarrays offer valuable insights into disease progression and personalized medicine.</p></p>
