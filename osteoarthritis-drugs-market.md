<p><p>The Osteoarthritis Drugs Market encompasses various types of medications used to manage osteoarthritis symptoms. Oral drugs are taken by mouth, such as pain relievers (acetaminophen, nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) and supplements (glucosamine, chondroitin). Injection drugs involve injecting medications directly into the affected joint, like corticosteroids or hyaluronic acid. External drugs refer to topical treatments like creams or gels that are applied directly to the skin over the affected joint, such as capsaicin or lidocaine patches. These different types of drugs offer options for individuals to alleviate pain and improve joint function in osteoarthritis.</p></p>
