<p><strong>The MEMS Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kelvin Testing</li><li>Four-probe Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS testing is a crucial process to assess the functionality and performance of microelectromechanical systems. Kelvin testing, a type of MEMS testing, measures electrical resistance accurately using four wire connections. It mitigates resistance errors caused by probe and contact resistance. On the other hand, four-probe testing is a technique employed to measure the resistance of microscopic structures within the MEMS device with high accuracy, ensuring reliable device operation. Both methods are important for quality control and optimization of MEMS devices before they are integrated into various applications.</p></p>
