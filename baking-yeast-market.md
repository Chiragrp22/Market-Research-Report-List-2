<p><p>The baking yeast market consists of two major types: regular active dry yeast and instant yeast. Regular active dry yeast is a popular type of yeast that requires proofing in warm water before use. It has a longer shelf life and is commonly used in traditional baking methods. On the other hand, instant yeast does not need to be proofed and can be directly incorporated into dough. It provides a faster rise and is convenient for faster baking processes, making it a preferred choice for many commercial bakers.</p></p>
