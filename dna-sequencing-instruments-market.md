<p><strong>The DNA Sequencing Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion PCR</li><li>Bridge Amplification</li><li>Single-molecule</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DNA sequencing instruments are used to determine the order of nucleotides (A, T, C, G) in a DNA molecule. Emulsion PCR is a technique that creates millions of DNA copies in tiny water droplets, allowing for parallel sequencing. Bridge amplification is a method where DNA fragments are attached to a solid surface and amplified to form a DNA bridge, enabling high-throughput sequencing. Single-molecule sequencing directly reads individual DNA strands, providing long read lengths. These methods contribute to the advancement of DNA sequencing technology, enabling accurate and efficient analysis of genetic information.</p></p>
