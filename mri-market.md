<p><strong>The MRI Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet MRI</li><li>Superconductive MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) is a medical imaging technology used to visualize internal body structures. The MRI market is divided into two primary types: Permanent Magnet MRI and Superconductive MRI. </p><p>Permanent Magnet MRI systems, as the name suggests, utilize a permanent magnet to generate a magnetic field. These systems are typically smaller in size, less expensive, and simpler to operate compared to Superconductive MRI systems. However, they provide lower image quality and are not suitable for advanced diagnostic imaging.</p><p>Superconductive MRI systems employ superconducting magnets, cooled by liquid helium, to generate a strong and stable magnetic field. These systems offer excellent image quality and are capable of performing advanced imaging techniques. However, they are larger, more expensive, and require specialized infrastructure and maintenance.</p></p>
