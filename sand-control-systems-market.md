<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The sand control systems market includes various types of systems that help prevent sand production during oil and gas production processes. Gravel pack systems involve placing a layer of gravel in the wellbore to filter out sand and prevent it from entering the production stream. Frac pack systems create fractures in the reservoir and place proppants to keep them open, reducing sand production. Sand screens are used to filter out sand particles while allowing oil and gas to flow through. Inflow control devices regulate the flow of fluids and prevent sand ingress into the wellbore.</p></p>
