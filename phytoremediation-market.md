<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Phytoremediation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phytosequestration</li><li>Rhizodegradation</li><li>Phytohydraulics</li><li>Phytoextraction</li><li>Phytovolatilization</li><li>Phytodegradation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phytoremediation encompasses various types of market segments. Phytosequestration involves plants absorbing and storing pollutants, while rhizodegradation focuses on plants promoting the breakdown of contaminants through root secretions. Phytohydraulics entails the use of plants to treat water bodies contaminated with pollutants. Phytoextraction involves plants absorbing and accumulating pollutants from the soil, while phytovolatilization occurs when plants take up pollutants and release them into the air. Lastly, phytodegradation involves plants breaking down contaminants through processes such as enzymatic reactions. These market types address different aspects of using plants for environmental remediation.</p></p>
