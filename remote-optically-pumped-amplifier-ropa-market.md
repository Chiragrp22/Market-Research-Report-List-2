<p><p>A Remote Optically Pumped Amplifier (ROPA) is a device used in optical communication systems to amplify optical signals over long distances. There are two types of ROPA markets: Same Fiber ROPA and Foreign Fiber ROPA. Same Fiber ROPA refers to amplifiers that are deployed in the same optical fiber as the signal being amplified. On the other hand, Foreign Fiber ROPA involves using a separate fiber for the amplifier and the signal. Both types play a crucial role in boosting optical signals and improving the efficiency of long-distance optical communication systems.</p></p>
