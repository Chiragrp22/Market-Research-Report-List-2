<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Electrical Resistance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic laboratory balance market includes various types, such as electromagnetic and electrical resistance balances. Electromagnetic balances measure the weight of an object using the force generated by an electromagnetic field. They are highly accurate and suitable for precision measurements in laboratory settings. Electrical resistance balances, on the other hand, rely on the change in electrical resistance to determine the weight of an object. They are commonly used for weighing purposes and offer good accuracy and precision. Both types serve different needs in laboratory environments, providing accurate and reliable measurements.</p></p>
