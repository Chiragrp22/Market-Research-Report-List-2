<p><p>Automatic milking machines are used in the dairy industry to streamline the milking process. There are two main types of automatic milking machines - Semi-Automatic and Fully-Automatic. Semi-Automatic milking machines require human intervention for attaching and detaching the milking cups, while the milking process itself is automated. On the other hand, Fully-Automatic milking machines operate without human intervention as they perform attachment, detachment, and the entire milking process automatically. Both types offer benefits such as increased efficiency, reduced labor costs, and improved milk quality.</p></p>
