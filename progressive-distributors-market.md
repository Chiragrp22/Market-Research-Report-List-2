<p><strong>The Progressive Distributors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VPA</li><li>VPB</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Progressive Distributors operates in two market types: VPA and VPB. VPA (Vendor-Provided Availability) market refers to situations where the distributor is responsible for maintaining inventory levels based on data provided by the vendor. This allows for efficient restocking and ensures products are readily available to customers. VPB (Vendor-Provided Backstock) market, on the other hand, means that the vendor is responsible for maintaining inventory levels at the distributor's warehouse. This arrangement minimizes supply chain complexity for the distributor and allows them to focus on sales and customer service.</p></p>
