<p><p>Rubber Tired Gantry (RTG) cranes are a type of mobile crane used for lifting and moving shipping containers at container terminals. These cranes are equipped with rubber tires that allow them to easily maneuver in the yard without the need for additional infrastructure, such as rails or tracks. They are commonly used in ports and intermodal terminals for stacking and retrieving containers from container stacks.</p><p>The Rubber Tired Gantry (RTG) Cranes Market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing volume of international trade and the growth in containerization have led to the expansion of container terminal operations, driving the demand for RTG cranes. Additionally, the need for efficient and productive operations in ports and terminals has further propelled market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the technological advancements in RTG cranes have contributed to their increased adoption. Companies are investing in automation and electrification technologies to improve the efficiency and reduce the emissions of these cranes, thus addressing environmental concerns and regulatory requirements.</p><p>The market is also witnessing various trends that are shaping its growth. For instance, the adoption of electric RTG cranes is increasing, driven by the need for sustainable and eco-friendly operations.
