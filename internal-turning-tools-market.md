<p><p>The internal turning tools market includes various types such as grooving, threading, end milling, and drilling tools. Grooving tools are used to create grooves or channels in a workpiece. Threading tools are used to cut threads inside a hole or on the surface of an object. End milling tools are designed for making slots or channels on the ends of workpieces. Drilling tools, on the other hand, are used to create holes in various materials. All these tools serve different purposes and cater to specific machining requirements.</p></p>
