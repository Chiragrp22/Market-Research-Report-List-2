Single-stage cordless snow blowers are compact and ideal for light snowfall on small surfaces. Two-stage cordless snow blowers are more powerful, with an auger that breaks up snow and a separate impeller that blows it out. They are suitable for heavier snowfall and larger areas. Three-stage cordless snow blowers are the most powerful, featuring an additional accelerator that assists in breaking up compacted snow and removing it efficiently.</p></p>
