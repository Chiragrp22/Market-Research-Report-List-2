<p><p>Optical Time Domain Reflectometers (OTDR) are advanced fiber optic testing instruments used to characterize and analyze fiber optic cables. OTDRs emit a series of light pulses into the fiber, measuring the time it takes for the backscattered or reflected light to return to the instrument. This data is then used to determine the quality, length, and location of faults in the fiber optic network.</p><p>The global OTDR market is projected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Several factors contribute to this growth, including the increasing demand for flawless and uninterrupted communication networks, rising investments in telecommunication infrastructure, and the surging adoption of fiber optic cables in various industry verticals.
