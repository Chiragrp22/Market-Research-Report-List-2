<p><ul><li>Portable Dust Monitor</li><li>Stationary Dust Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dust monitor market can be categorized into two types: portable dust monitors and stationary dust monitors. Portable dust monitors are handheld devices used to measure and monitor the concentration of dust particles in the air. They are lightweight and easy to carry, making them suitable for on-the-go monitoring. On the other hand, stationary dust monitors are fixed in a specific location and continuously measure and record dust levels over an extended period. They are commonly used for long-term monitoring in industrial plants, construction sites, or other areas requiring constant monitoring of air quality.</p></p>
