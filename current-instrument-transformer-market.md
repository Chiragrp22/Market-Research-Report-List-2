<p><p>The current instrument transformer market consists of four major types: dry type current transformers, pouring type current transformers, oil-immersed current transformers, and gas-insulated current transformers. Dry type current transformers use resin for insulation, while pouring type transformers use epoxy resin. Oil-immersed transformers are filled with oil for insulation, and gas-insulated transformers use gas as an insulating medium. These different types cater to specific applications and environments, providing accurate measurement and safe operation of electrical currents.</p></p>
