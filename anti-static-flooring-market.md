<p><ul><li>Resilient Anti-Static Flooring</li><li>Non-Resilient Anti-Static Flooring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-static flooring market can be categorized into two types: resilient anti-static flooring and non-resilient anti-static flooring. Resilient anti-static flooring refers to floors made of materials like vinyl, rubber, or linoleum, which offer flexibility and shock absorption. Non-resilient anti-static flooring, on the other hand, includes floors made of hard materials like epoxy or ceramic, which provide durability and resistance to heavy loads. Both types of flooring are designed to minimize or eliminate static electricity in environments where electrostatic discharge can be damaging to sensitive equipment or hazardous for people.</p></p>
