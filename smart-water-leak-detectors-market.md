<p><ul><li>Passive Leak Detector</li><li>Active Leak Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart water leak detectors are devices used to detect leaks and prevent water damage in homes and buildings. There are two main types of smart water leak detectors: passive leak detectors and active leak detectors. </p><p>Passive leak detectors are placed near potential water leak sources and are activated when they come into contact with water. They rely on the water's conductivity to trigger an alarm or send a notification to the user.</p><p>On the other hand, active leak detectors continuously monitor the water system and use sensors to detect changes in water flow or pressure. They can proactively detect leaks and automatically shut off the water supply to prevent further damage.</p><p>Both types aim to provide early detection of leaks, allowing prompt action to be taken to minimize water damage and reduce repair costs.</p></p>
