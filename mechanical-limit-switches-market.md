<p><ul><li>Multiple Mechanical Limit Switches</li><li>Single Mechanical Limit Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mechanical Limit Switches market consists of two types: Multiple Mechanical Limit Switches and Single Mechanical Limit Switches. Multiple Mechanical Limit Switches are designed to have multiple actuators that can be triggered from different directions, allowing for more diverse sensing capabilities. On the other hand, Single Mechanical Limit Switches have only one actuator, limiting their sensing range to a specific direction. Both types play a crucial role in various industrial applications, providing accurate position sensing and ensuring safety operations.</p></p>
