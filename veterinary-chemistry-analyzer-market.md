<p><p>The Veterinary Chemistry Analyzer market consists of two types: Fully Automatic and Semi-automatic. Fully Automatic analyzers perform all tasks, including sample preparation, mixing, testing, and result analysis, without human intervention, providing fast and accurate results. On the other hand, Semi-automatic analyzers require some degree of human involvement, such as sample loading and result interpretation, but still offer efficient testing capabilities.
