<p><strong>The Metal Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Articulated Robots</li><li>Cartesian Robots</li><li>SCARA Robots</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal robots, also known as industrial robots, can be classified into various market types based on their structures and capabilities. Articulated robots feature a series of connected joints that create a versatile range of motions, allowing them to access complex spaces. Cartesian robots move along linear axes, making them ideal for handling heavy loads in a specific direction. SCARA robots, on the other hand, have both rotary and linear joints, providing high speed and precision for assembly tasks. Lastly, the "Other" market type encompasses robots with unique designs or hybrid robot systems that do not fit into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
