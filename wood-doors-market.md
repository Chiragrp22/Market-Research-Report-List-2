<p><p>The wood doors market consists of three main types: solid wood doors, wood composite doors, and molded doors. Solid wood doors are made entirely from a single piece of natural wood, offering a traditional and luxurious aesthetic. Wood composite doors are made from a combination of wood particles and synthetic materials, providing durability and affordability. Molded doors are made by pressing wood fibers and resins together, resulting in a lightweight and cost-effective option. Each type of wood door caters to different customer preferences, budgets, and requirements.</p></p>
