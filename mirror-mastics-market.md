<p><p>Mirror mastics are adhesive substances used for securing mirrors to walls or other surfaces. There are three main types of mirror mastics available in the market. Neutral mirror mastics have a neutral pH level and are commonly used for regular mirror installations. Acid mirror mastics, on the other hand, have a higher acidic content and are specifically designed for use in humid or wet environments to prevent mirror deterioration. Apart from these two, there are other market variations of mirror mastics that cater to specialized needs, such as mirror mastic with enhanced bonding strength or fire-resistant properties.</p></p>
