<p><strong>The Intelligent Gas Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm</li><li>Turbine</li><li>Rotary piston</li><li>Thermal mass flow</li><li>Ultrasonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intelligent gas meter market is categorized into five types: diaphragm, turbine, rotary piston, thermal mass flow, and ultrasonic meters. Diaphragm meters use flexible membranes to measure gas flow, while turbine meters employ a spinning rotor to measure gas consumption. Rotary piston meters use rotating pistons to quantify gas usage, and thermal mass flow meters measure gas flow based on the cooling effect of the moving gas. Lastly, ultrasonic meters use sound waves to determine gas flow rates. These different types cater to the diverse needs and preferences of gas consumers and industries.</p></p>
