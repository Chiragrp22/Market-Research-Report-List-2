<p><p>The cement mixer market can be categorized into two main types: mixing trucks and small portable mixers. Mixing trucks are large vehicles that are specifically designed to transport and mix cement on construction sites. They have a rotating drum that mixes the cement ingredients while in transit. On the other hand, small portable mixers are compact machines that can be easily transported to different locations. They are typically used for smaller construction projects or for do-it-yourself projects where a smaller amount of cement is needed.</p></p>
