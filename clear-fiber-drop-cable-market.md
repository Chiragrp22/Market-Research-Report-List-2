<p><p>The Clear Fiber Drop Cable market consists of two main types: Single Mode and Multimode cables. Single Mode cables allow for transmission of a single mode of light, typically used for long-distance data transmission. It provides the highest signal quality and bandwidth, making it ideal for applications where data travels over lengthy distances. On the other hand, Multimode cables enable the transmission of multiple modes of light simultaneously. They are commonly used for shorter distance transmission within buildings or local networks, offering a more cost-effective solution for high-speed data transfer.</p></p>
