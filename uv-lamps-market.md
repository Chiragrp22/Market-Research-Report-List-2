<p><p>The UV lamp market can be classified into two types based on the materials used for lamp construction: High Boron Glass Lamps and Quartz Glass Lamps. High Boron Glass Lamps are made from a type of glass that contains a high concentration of boron, which allows them to emit UV light efficiently. Quartz Glass Lamps, on the other hand, are made from high-purity quartz glass, which has excellent UV transmittance properties. Both types of lamps are used in various applications that require UV radiation, such as sterilization, water treatment, and phototherapy.</p></p>
