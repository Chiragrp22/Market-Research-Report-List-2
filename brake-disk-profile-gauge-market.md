<p><strong>The Brake Disk Profile Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wheel Mounted</li><li>Axle Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Brake Disk Profile Gauge market consists of two main types: Wheel Mounted and Axle Mounted. </p><p>1. Wheel Mounted gauges are attached directly to the wheel assembly of a vehicle, allowing for real-time measurements of the brake disk profile while the vehicle is in motion. This type of gauge provides accurate and continuous readings, making it ideal for monitoring brake performance and detecting potential issues during operation.</p><p>2. Axle Mounted gauges, on the other hand, are installed on the axle assembly of a vehicle. They provide measurements of the brake disk profile when the vehicle is stationary. This type of gauge is commonly used during maintenance or inspection procedures to assess the condition of the brake system and ensure proper disk profile.</p></p>
