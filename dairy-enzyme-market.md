<p><p>The Dairy Enzyme Market comprises various types of enzymes used in the dairy industry. Lactase is an enzyme that breaks down lactose into simpler sugars, making it suitable for lactose-intolerant individuals. Chymosin, also known as rennet, is used in cheese production to curdle milk. Microbial rennet is a vegetarian alternative to animal-derived chymosin. Lipase is an enzyme added to cheese for flavor development. Besides these, there are other enzymes like protease and xylanase used for various dairy applications, such as milk protein hydrolysis and improving texture in yogurt and fermented milk products.</p></p>
