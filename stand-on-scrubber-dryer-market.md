<p><p>The stand-on scrubber dryer market consists of two main types: disk brush scrubber dryers and cylindrical brush scrubber dryers. Disk brush scrubber dryers utilize a round brush that rotates and scrubs the floor surface, while cylindrical brush scrubber dryers use a cylindrical brush that rotates and simultaneously sweeps debris into a built-in hopper. Both types are designed for larger cleaning areas and offer improved maneuverability due to their stand-on design. They are commonly used in commercial and industrial settings to effectively clean and dry floors.</p></p>
