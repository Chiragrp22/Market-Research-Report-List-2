<p><p>Adhesion testers are used to measure the adhesion strength between two materials. There are various types of adhesion testers available in the market. Tensile testing machines assess the force required to separate the materials, while friction testing machines measure the resistance encountered when two objects slide against each other. Peel testing machines evaluate the strength needed to peel materials apart. Multifunctional testing machines can perform multiple adhesion tests, providing more versatility. Additionally, there are other types of adhesion testers available, all designed to assess the adhesive properties of different materials.</p></p>
