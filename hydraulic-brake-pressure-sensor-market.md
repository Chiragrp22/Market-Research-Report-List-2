<p><ul><li>Analog Output</li><li>Digital Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic brake pressure sensor market consists of two types: analog output and digital output. Analog output sensors provide a continuous range of pressure values in the form of a voltage or current signal. This type of sensor is commonly used in older vehicles and industrial applications. On the other hand, digital output sensors provide discrete pressure values in a binary code format. They are commonly found in modern vehicles and offer better precision and compatibility with digital systems. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring accurate brake pressure measurements for efficient braking systems.</p></p>
