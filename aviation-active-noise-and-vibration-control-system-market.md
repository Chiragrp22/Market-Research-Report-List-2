<p><p>The Aviation Active Noise Control System Market includes active noise control systems and active vibration control systems. Active noise control systems help reduce unwanted noise in aircraft cabins by emitting anti-noise waves that cancel out the noise. These systems use microphones and speakers to detect and produce sound waves to counteract the cabin noise.
