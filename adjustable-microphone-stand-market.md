Tripod stands feature three legs that offer enhanced stability and support for heavier microphones during performances or presentations. They are popular in live music settings and are easily adjustable to accommodate different heights and angles. Both types aim to securely hold microphones and minimize unwanted vibrations for optimal audio recording or amplification purposes.</p></p>
