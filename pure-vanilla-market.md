<p><p>The Pure Vanilla market encompasses four main types: Bean, Extract, Powder, and Paste. Vanilla Beans are the whole, natural form of vanilla, often used for infusing flavors. Vanilla Extract is made by soaking vanilla beans in alcohol, resulting in a highly concentrated liquid flavoring. Vanilla Powder is produced by drying and grinding vanilla beans, providing a convenient powdered form. Vanilla Paste contains ground vanilla beans combined with sugar and sometimes corn syrup, offering a thicker consistency and intense flavor. These variations cater to different preferences and culinary needs, allowing consumers to choose the suitable option for their recipes.</p></p>
