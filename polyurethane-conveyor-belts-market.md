<p><p>Polyurethane conveyor belts are used in various industries for efficient material handling. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) conveyor belts are known for their high flexibility and abrasion resistance, making them suitable for applications where small pulley diameters are required. PUR (Polyurethane) conveyor belts have enhanced resistance to chemicals, oils, and heat, making them suitable for industries like food processing and automotive. Both types offer excellent durability and longevity, enabling smooth and reliable conveying operations. These qualities make TPU and PUR conveyor belts popular choices in the market for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
