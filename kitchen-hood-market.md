<p><strong>The Kitchen Hood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Under Cabinet Mount</li><li>Wall Mount</li><li>Ceiling (Island) Mount</li><li>Downdraft Ventilation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kitchen hood market is divided into different types based on their installation options. Under Cabinet Mount hoods are installed beneath kitchen cabinets to save space. Wall Mount hoods are mounted on the wall and are a popular choice for traditional kitchen setups. Ceiling (Island) Mount hoods hang from the ceiling over kitchen islands or peninsula countertops, offering a stylish and efficient solution. Downdraft Ventilation hoods are installed flush with the cooktop and pull air downwards, making them suitable for kitchen setups without overhead cabinetry or limited space.</p></p>
