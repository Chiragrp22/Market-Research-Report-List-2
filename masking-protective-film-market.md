<p><p>Masking Protective Film is a versatile solution used in various industries to protect surfaces from damage during manufacturing, transportation, or installation processes. There are two main types available: Solid Masking Protective Film and Liquid Masking Protective Film. </p><p>Solid Masking Protective Film is a peelable film that adheres to surfaces, protecting them from scratches, dust, and debris. It can be easily applied and removed without leaving residue. </p><p>On the other hand, Liquid Masking Protective Film is applied as a liquid coating that dries into a protective film. It provides a seamless and flexible barrier against damages. Both types offer excellent protection, allowing for efficient and damage-free operations in different sectors.</p></p>
