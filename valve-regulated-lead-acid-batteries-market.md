<p><p>The Valve Regulated Lead Acid Batteries market includes two main types: Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries are constructed with a glass mat separator that absorbs the battery's electrolyte, making it less prone to spills and leaks. They offer high power density, outstanding cyclic performance, and are suitable for various applications. Gel batteries, on the other hand, utilize a gel electrolyte, which immobilizes the acid and prevents leakage. They provide excellent deep cycle performance, longer lifespan, and are commonly used in sensitive applications like medical equipment and telecommunications.</p></p>
