<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure</li><li>Negative Pressure</li><li>Neutral Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Needle-free connectors are medical devices used to connect catheters or IV lines without the need for a needle. They are divided into three types based on their pressure characteristics: positive pressure, negative pressure, and neutral pressure connectors. </p><p>Positive pressure connectors prevent blood reflux and air entry by using a mechanism that maintains a continuous positive pressure within the connector. Negative pressure connectors use a mechanism that creates a vacuum within the connector, preventing blood leakage and air entry. Neutral pressure connectors have a pressure-balancing mechanism that equalizes the pressure and facilitates the smooth infusion of fluids. Each type of connector offers specific advantages and is chosen based on the clinical needs of the patient and healthcare facility.</p></p>
