<p><p>A Solar Home System (SHS) refers to a photovoltaic (PV) system that generates electricity using solar energy for various residential applications. SHSs are widely used in areas without access to grid electricity or unstable grid connections, particularly in developing countries.</p><p>In terms of market growth analysis, the Solar Home System Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include increasing awareness of renewable energy sources, rising utility costs, government initiatives promoting solar energy, and technological advancements in the PV industry. Energy storage enables efficient use of solar energy, especially during non-sunlight hours. Pay-as-you-go models allow affordable access to solar energy for low-income households by offering flexible payment options.
