<p><p>Laser acupuncture is a growing market that offers different types of laser sources for effective treatment. Carbon source lasers utilize carbon rods to emit infrared light, while He-Ne light source lasers use helium and neon gases to generate red light. Semiconductor lasers are compact and generate low-level laser beams, while Nd: YAG lasers offer high power and precise treatment by using neodymium-doped yttrium aluminum garnet crystals. Each type of laser source provides unique benefits and options for practitioners to cater to various treatment requirements in the field of acupuncture.</p></p>
