<p><p>Cooling towers are a type of heat exchange system used in industries to remove excess heat from a process. There are three main types of cooling towers in the market. Evaporative cooling towers use water and air to cool down the process. Dry cooling towers use only air to cool the process, making them suitable for areas with water scarcity. Hybrid cooling towers combine both water and air cooling methods for more efficient heat transfer. These cooling towers cater to different needs and environmental conditions, providing options for various industries.</p></p>
