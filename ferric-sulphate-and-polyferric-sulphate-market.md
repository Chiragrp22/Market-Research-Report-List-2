<p><p>Ferric sulphate and polyferric sulphate are chemical compounds used in various industries for water treatment and wastewater treatment processes. </p><p>Ferric sulphate, also known as iron(III) sulphate, is a yellow hygroscopic solid that is commonly used as a coagulant in wastewater treatment plants. It helps in the removal of impurities by coagulating small particles into larger ones, facilitating their separation from the water. It is also used in the treatment of drinking water to remove color, odour, and turbidity.</p><p>Polyferric sulphate, on the other hand, is a high-performance inorganic polymer coagulant that consists of multiple ferric iron ions cross-linked with sulphate. It is commonly used as a replacement for traditional coagulants like aluminium and ferrous salts due to its superior coagulation efficiency, strong adaptability, and low dosage requirements. Polyferric sulphate is widely used in industries such as wastewater treatment, papermaking, and textile.</p><p>The Ferric Sulphate and Polyferric Sulphate Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for water treatment solutions worldwide.
