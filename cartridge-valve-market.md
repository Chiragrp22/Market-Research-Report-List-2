<p><strong>The Cartridge Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw-in Cartridge Valve</li><li>Slip-in Cartridge Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cartridge valves are hydraulic components used in various machinery to control the flow and pressure of fluids. There are two main types: screw-in cartridge valves and slip-in cartridge valves. Screw-in cartridge valves are threaded and installed directly into the hydraulic manifold, making them more secure and resistant to vibration. Slip-in cartridge valves slide into a machined cavity within the manifold, offering flexibility in design and easy replacement. Both types provide efficient fluid control, enhancing the performance and reliability of hydraulic systems in a wide range of applications.</p></p>
