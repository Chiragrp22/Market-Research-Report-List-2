<p><p>Worm geared motors are a type of power transmission system used in various industrial applications. There are two main types of worm geared motors: helical-worm and double-worm. </p><p>A helical-worm geared motor combines the features of helical and worm gearsets. It offers enhanced efficiency, higher torque transmission capabilities, and smoother operation. This type of gear motor is commonly used in conveyor systems, elevators, and mixers.</p><p>On the other hand, a double-worm geared motor utilizes two worm gearsets in a single unit, resulting in increased torque output and improved load distribution. It is suitable for heavy-duty applications that require high torque, such as cranes, winches, and mining equipment.</p><p>Both helical-worm and double-worm geared motors provide reliable and efficient power transmission solutions, but their specific characteristics make them suitable for different applications within the industrial sector.</p></p>
