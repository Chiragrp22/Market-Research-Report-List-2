<p><p>The forklift ramp market consists of two main types: manual forklift ramps and automatic forklift ramps. Manual forklift ramps require physical labor to set up and use, usually by pushing or pulling the ramp into place. They are generally more affordable and simple to operate. On the other hand, automatic forklift ramps are motorized and can be controlled remotely or with the push of a button. They are typically more expensive but offer convenience and efficiency in loading and unloading operations.</p></p>
