<p><strong>The Methyl Methacrylate Monomer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ACH Method</li><li>Isobutylene Method</li><li>Ethylene Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Methyl Methacrylate Monomer (MMA) market is categorized into three types: ACH Method, Isobutylene Method, and Ethylene Method. The ACH Method involves the reaction between acetone, hydrogen cyanide, and hydrogen sulfide to produce MMA. Isobutylene Method utilizes the reaction of isobutyl alcohol and formaldehyde to obtain MMA. Lastly, Ethylene Method involves the oxidation of ethylene to produce MMA. These different methods cater to varying manufacturing demands and have their own advantages and limitations in terms of efficiency, cost, and environmental impact.</p></p>
