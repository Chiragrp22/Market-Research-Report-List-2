Front silver paste is used as a conductive layer on the front side of the solar cell, helping to collect sunlight and convert it into electricity. Back silver paste is applied on the back side to form electrical contacts, while aluminum paste is an alternative to silver paste, offering cost advantages but slightly lower efficiency.</p></p>
