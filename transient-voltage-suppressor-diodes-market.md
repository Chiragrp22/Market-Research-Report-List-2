<p><p>The transient voltage suppressor diodes market can be categorized into two main types: unipolar TVS diodes and bipolar TVS diodes. Unipolar TVS diodes are designed to clamp the voltage of a circuit to a specific level by effectively short-circuiting transient overvoltages. On the other hand, bipolar TVS diodes utilize both positive and negative voltage polarities to suppress transient overvoltages. Both types are commonly used in electronic devices to protect sensitive components from voltage spikes and surges, ensuring their safe operation and extending their lifespan.</p></p>
