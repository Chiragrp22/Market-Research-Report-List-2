<p><p>Vertical electrophoresis systems refer to laboratory equipment used to separate and analyze DNA, RNA, and proteins. Two common types of these systems are Agarose Gel Electrophoresis System and Polyacrylamide Gel Electrophoresis System. Agarose gel systems are cost-effective and suitable for larger DNA fragments, while polyacrylamide gel systems offer higher resolution for smaller molecules like proteins. Both systems utilize an electric field to move charged particles through a gel matrix, allowing scientists to visualize and analyze biomolecules based on their size and charge.</p></p>
