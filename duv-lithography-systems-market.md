<p><p>DUV lithography systems are used in the semiconductor industry for patterning integrated circuits. ArF Dry Excimer Systems, ArF Immersion Excimer Systems, and KrF Excimer Systems are three types of DUV lithography systems. ArF Dry Excimer Systems use an argon fluoride (ArF) excimer laser in a dry environment. ArF Immersion Excimer Systems use the same type of laser but in combination with immersion lithography techniques. KrF Excimer Systems use a krypton fluoride (KrF) excimer laser. These different types cater to varying requirements and preferences in the market.</p></p>
