<p><p>The building materials industry utilizes various types of conveyor belts for smooth material handling. These include TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane), TPEE (thermoplastic polyester elastomer), PVC (polyvinyl chloride), PE (polyethylene), and others. TPU belts boast high tensile strength and excellent resistance to oil and abrasion. TPEE belts offer good flexibility and elasticity, making them suitable for moderate usage. PVC belts exhibit durability, chemical resistance, and fire-retardant properties. PE belts are lightweight, non-toxic, and resistant to moisture. The "others" category includes specialty belts designed for specific applications such as high-temperature resistance or anti-static properties.</p></p>
