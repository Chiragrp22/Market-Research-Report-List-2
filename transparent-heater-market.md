<p><p>Transparent heaters are an emerging market with two main types: transparent flexible heaters and nonflexible transparent heaters. Transparent flexible heaters are thin and bendable, making them suitable for applications requiring conformability. They can be easily integrated into various devices like touchscreens, car windows, or wearable electronics. On the other hand, nonflexible transparent heaters lack the flexibility of their counterparts but provide excellent transparency and heat conduction. Thus, they are commonly used in applications like display panels, sensors, or laboratory equipment. Both types of transparent heaters offer distinct advantages depending on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
