<p><p>A thermal bypass valve is a device used in internal combustion engines to regulate the flow of coolant through the engine's cooling system. It is typically found in vehicles with liquid cooling systems, such as cars and trucks. The valve bypasses the flow of coolant around the engine until it reaches its optimal operating temperature, at which point it opens to allow coolant to flow through the engine.</p><p>The global thermal bypass valve market is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period.
