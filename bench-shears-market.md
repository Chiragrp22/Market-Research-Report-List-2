<p><p>The bench shears market consists of two main types: straight blade bench shears and rotary blade bench shears. Straight blade bench shears are designed with a fixed blade that moves against a stationary one, enabling precise and clean cuts on various materials. On the other hand, rotary blade bench shears employ a rotating blade to cut through materials smoothly and effortlessly. Both types cater to different needs, with straight blade shears offering accuracy and rotary blade shears providing ease of use.</p></p>
