<p><p>Named Driver Insurance is a type of auto insurance policy that covers specific individuals designated as named drivers. Unlike traditional insurance policies that cover the vehicle owner, this policy allows individuals who do not own the vehicle to be insured while driving it. Typically, the named driver is required to be nominated by the vehicle owner at the time of purchasing the policy.</p><p>The market for Named Driver Insurance is expected to experience substantial growth in the forecast period. One of the major factors driving this growth is the increasing number of households with multiple drivers but only one vehicle. Named Driver Insurance offers an affordable alternative to insuring additional drivers on the same vehicle, making it an attractive option for cost-conscious individuals.</p><p>Additionally, the rise in ride-sharing services has also contributed to the demand for Named Driver Insurance. As more individuals participate in ride-sharing, they often require temporary insurance coverage while using a borrowed or rented vehicle. Named Driver Insurance provides a flexible solution for these individuals, allowing them to be protected on the road.</p><p>Moreover, the convenience and ease of purchasing Named Driver Insurance policies online have boosted market growth.
