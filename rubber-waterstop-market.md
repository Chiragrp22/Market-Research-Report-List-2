<p><p>The rubber waterstop market includes various types, such as Dumbbell Type and Center Bulb Type. The Dumbbell Type rubber waterstop is shaped like a barbell, with two larger ends and a narrower middle section, allowing for expansion and contraction of concrete joints. On the other hand, the Center Bulb Type features a centered bulb-like structure that provides superior water-tightness by sealing construction joints. Both types serve as crucial components in concrete structures, preventing water infiltration and leakage.</p></p>
