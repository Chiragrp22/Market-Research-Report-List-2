<p><strong>The Grinder Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-positive displacement (SPD) Grinder Pumps</li><li>Centrifugal Grinder Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi-positive displacement (SPD) Grinder Pumps are a type of grinder pump that operate by forcing wastewater through the pump using a piston or rotating blades. They are known for their high pressure capabilities and ability to handle tough solid waste.</p><p>Centrifugal Grinder Pumps, on the other hand, utilize centrifugal force to move wastewater through the pump. These pumps have a rotating impeller that propels the wastewater outward and creates pressure, allowing for efficient pumping of solids and liquids.</p><p>Both types of grinder pumps are commonly used in residential and commercial applications to effectively manage sewage and wastewater.</p></p>
