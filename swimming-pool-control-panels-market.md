<p><p>Swimming Pool Control Panels market can be divided into two types: fixed control panels and mobile control panels. Fixed control panels are permanently installed near the swimming pool area and are typically wired into the pool's electrical system. They provide control and monitoring of various pool functions, such as temperature, filtration, lighting, and water chemistry. On the other hand, mobile control panels are portable devices that can be easily moved around the pool area. They offer similar control and monitoring features but are not permanently installed, providing more flexibility and convenience for pool owners.</p></p>
