<p><ul><li>Security Camera Fixed Lens</li><li>Security Camera Varifocal Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of security lenses in the market: fixed lens and varifocal lens for security cameras. Fixed lenses have a fixed focal length, meaning they provide a specific field of view and cannot be adjusted. On the other hand, varifocal lenses allow for manual adjustment of focal length, providing flexibility to modify the field of view. Both types of lenses serve different purposes in security surveillance, with fixed lenses offering consistent coverage, and varifocal lenses offering adjustable perspectives.</p></p>
