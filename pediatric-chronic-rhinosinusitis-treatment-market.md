<p><p>Pediatric Chronic Rhinosinusitis (PCR) refers to the inflammation of the nasal sinuses and nasal passages in children that lasts for a prolonged period of time, typically more than 12 weeks. The treatment for Pediatric Chronic Rhinosinusitis involves a combination of medications, surgery, and other supportive measures.</p><p>The primary goal of treatment is to relieve symptoms, reduce inflammation, and prevent recurrence.
