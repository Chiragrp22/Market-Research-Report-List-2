Manufacturers are investing in research and development activities to enhance the wear resistance and longevity of mill liners.</p><p>Furthermore, the advent of smart liners that are embedded with sensors and monitoring systems is gaining traction in the market. These replacement liners offer an alternative option for mill operators and are often more cost-effective.</p></p>
