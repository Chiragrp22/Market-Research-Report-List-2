<p><p>Shade devices market can be classified into two types based on their connectivity options: Wi-Fi and BLE. Wi-Fi refers to devices that connect to the internet wirelessly, allowing users to control them remotely through an internet connection. These devices can be operated using smartphones or other smart devices from anywhere in the world. BLE, short for Bluetooth Low Energy, allows devices to communicate wirelessly over short distances. BLE shade devices can be controlled locally using smartphones or tablets that are in close proximity, typically within a range of 100 meters.</p></p>
