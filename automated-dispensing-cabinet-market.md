<p><p>Automated Dispensing Cabinet (ADC) is a technology used in healthcare settings to securely manage the storage and distribution of medications. There are three main types of ADCs: </p><p>1. Tablet ADCs: These cabinets store tablets or pills in individual cells, providing precise automated dispensing based on prescribed dosages for patients. </p><p>2. Dose ADCs: These cabinets store pre-packaged doses of medications in a unit-dose format, ready for distribution directly to patients, nurses, or physicians. </p><p>3. Vial ADCs: These cabinets store vials of medications, allowing healthcare professionals to withdraw specific quantities of medication for individual patients, ensuring accurate and efficient dispensing.</p></p>
