<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The ETC System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rfid-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Dsrc-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Gnss/Gps-Based ETC Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ETC (Electronic Toll Collection) system market is classified into four types: RFID-based, DSRC-based, video analytics-based, and GNSS/GPS-based systems. RFID-based systems use radio frequency identification technology for toll collection. DSRC-based systems use Dedicated Short Range Communication to enable communication between the toll booth and a transponder in the vehicle. Video analytic-based systems use cameras and image processing techniques to capture the license plate and calculate tolls. Lastly, GNSS/GPS-based systems use positioning technology to determine the vehicle's location and calculate tolls accordingly. Each market type utilizes different technologies for effective toll collection.</p></p>
