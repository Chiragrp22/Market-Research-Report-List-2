<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Fiber optic sensors are classified into four types based on the modulation technique used: intensity modulated fiber optics sensors, phase modulated fiber optics sensors, wavelength modulated fiber optics sensors, and polarization modulated fiber optics sensors. </p><p>Intensity modulated sensors measure changes in light intensity traveling through the fiber to detect variations in physical quantities. Phase modulated sensors utilize changes in the phase of the light to measure physical parameters. Wavelength modulated sensors use the change in wavelength to detect variations. Polarization modulated sensors utilize changes in light polarization to measure physical quantities. These sensors find applications in various industries for precise and accurate measurements.</p></p>
