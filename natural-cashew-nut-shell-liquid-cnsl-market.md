<p><p>Natural Cashew Nut Shell Liquid (CNSL) is a dark reddish-brown viscous liquid extracted from cashew nut shells. The market is expected to witness a significant growth rate during the forecasted period. The increasing demand for CNSL in various end-use industries is one of the key factors driving the market growth. CNSL is extensively used as a raw material in the production of friction materials such as brake pads and linings, which are in high demand due to the growing automotive industry globally. Additionally, CNSL's applications in the resin industry, where it is used as a bio-based resin modifier, are also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, CNSL is gaining popularity in the cosmetic industry due to its antioxidant properties and use as a natural ingredient in skincare products.
