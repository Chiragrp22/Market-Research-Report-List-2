<p><p>Finishing machinery market types can be categorized into wet technologies and dry technologies. Wet technologies involve the use of liquids, such as water or chemical solutions, for processes like washing, coating, or dyeing. These technologies are commonly used in textile or printing industries. On the other hand, dry technologies rely on techniques like heat, friction, or air, which do not involve the use of liquids. These technologies are often used in industries like woodworking, metalworking, or electronics manufacturing. Both wet and dry technologies cater to different industries and provide specific finishing solutions.</p></p>
