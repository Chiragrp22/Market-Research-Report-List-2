<p><p>The Robot Pool Cleaner market is divided into two main types: Corded and Cordless. Corded Robot Pool Cleaners are connected to a power source through a cord, which limits their range but ensures continuous operation. On the other hand, Cordless Robot Pool Cleaners operate on rechargeable batteries, providing greater flexibility and freedom of movement. Both types offer efficient cleaning for pools, but Cordless models are preferred for their portability and ability to navigate obstacles without tangling cords.</p></p>
