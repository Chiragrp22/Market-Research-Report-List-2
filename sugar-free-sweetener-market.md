Firstly, rising concerns about obesity and related health issues have prompted consumers to seek healthier alternatives to sugar. The convenience and flexibility of using sugar-free sweeteners in cooking and baking have also contributed to market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology and the development of innovative sweeteners have expanded the market.
