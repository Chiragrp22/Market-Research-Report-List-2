<p><p>The patient cooling system market consists of three main types: surface cooling systems, intravascular cooling systems, and cooling accessories. Surface cooling systems involve the use of cooling blankets or pads placed on the patient's skin to reduce body temperature. Intravascular cooling systems involve the insertion of catheters to cool the patient's blood internally. Cooling accessories refer to additional devices or products used in conjunction with the cooling systems, such as power supply units, control units, or temperature monitors.</p></p>
