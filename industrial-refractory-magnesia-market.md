<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The industrial refractory magnesia market comprises three main types: fused magnesia, dead burned magnesia, and others. Fused magnesia is produced by melting magnesite in an electric arc furnace, resulting in a dense, high-quality product. Dead burned magnesia is created through the heating of magnesite at high temperatures, leading to a dense and chemically stable material. The "others" category includes magnesium hydroxide, caustic calcined magnesia, and others. Each type of magnesia has diverse applications in industrial refractory materials, providing high temperatures resistance and excellent insulation.</p></p>
