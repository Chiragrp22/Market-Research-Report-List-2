<p><p>The water heater repair market consists of two main types: tank storage and tankless. In the tank storage market, water heaters store and heat a specific amount of water in a tank, supplying it for various household needs. Tankless water heaters, on the other hand, heat water only when needed, providing continuous hot water without the need for a storage tank. Both markets cater to the demand for repairing these types of water heaters, with technicians specializing in fixing and maintaining their respective systems.</p></p>
