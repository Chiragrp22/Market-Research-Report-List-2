<p><p>Website monitoring services can be divided into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise monitoring involves installing software on local servers, allowing businesses to have complete control over the monitoring process. It can require more resources and IT expertise, but offers greater customization and security. On the other hand, cloud-based monitoring relies on remote servers provided by a third-party service. It is more accessible and scalable, with the service provider handling maintenance and updates. While it may lack some customization options, it offers convenience and flexibility.</p></p>
