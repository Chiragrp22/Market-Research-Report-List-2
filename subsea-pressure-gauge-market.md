<p><p>In the subsea pressure gauge market, there are two main types: Compensated Model and Not Compensated Model. </p><p>The Compensated Model pressure gauge is designed to withstand extreme subsea conditions by utilizing a compensation mechanism to counteract the effects of temperature changes and hydrostatic pressure. This ensures accurate and reliable pressure readings.</p><p>On the other hand, the Not Compensated Model does not have a compensation mechanism and is more suitable for applications where temperature and pressure fluctuations are not significant. However, it may be more prone to inaccuracies under varying subsea conditions.</p></p>
