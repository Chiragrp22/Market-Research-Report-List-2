<p><p>Road tampers are machines used for compacting and leveling the soil or aggregate while constructing or repairing roads. The market for road tampers can be classified into three types: multi-purpose, points and crossing, and straight track. Multi-purpose road tampers are versatile and can be used for various road projects. Points and crossing road tampers are specifically designed for the construction and maintenance of railway crossings. Straight track road tampers focus on compacting the soil or aggregate on straight sections of railway tracks. These different types cater to specific needs in the road construction industry.</p></p>
