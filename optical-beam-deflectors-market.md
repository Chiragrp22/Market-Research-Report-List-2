<p><strong>The Optical Beam Deflectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acousto-optic Deflectors</li><li>Electro-Optic Deflectors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical beam deflectors are devices used to change the direction of light beams. Acousto-optic deflectors utilize sound waves to diffract and deflect light, allowing for high-speed scanning without mechanical components. Electro-optic deflectors, on the other hand, rely on an electric field to alter the refractive index of a material, changing the beam's path. Other types of beam deflectors may include devices based on diffraction gratings or microelectromechanical systems. Each type of deflector offers unique advantages and applications in fields such as laser scanning, microscopy, and telecommunications.</p></p>
