<p><strong>The Ceramic Variable Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Ceramic Variable Capacitors</li><li>DIP Ceramic Variable Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceramic Variable Capacitors market is segmented into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) Ceramic Variable Capacitors and DIP (Dual In-line Package) Ceramic Variable Capacitors. SMD Ceramic Variable Capacitors are small-sized components that can be directly mounted on printed circuit boards, making them suitable for compact electronic devices. On the other hand, DIP Ceramic Variable Capacitors are larger in size and have pins on both sides for through-hole mounting, making them ideal for applications where a larger capacitance range is required. Both types are commonly used in various electronic devices for tuning circuits and adjusting frequency.</p></p>
