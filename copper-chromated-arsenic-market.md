<p><p>The Copper Chromated Arsenic (CCA) market consists of two main types: CCA-C and Others. CCA-C refers to the primary type of CCA, which is commonly used as a wood preservative. It contains copper, chromium, and arsenic compounds to protect wood from rotting and insect damage. On the other hand, the Others market includes alternative wood preservatives that do not contain arsenic, such as alkaline copper quaternary (ACQ) and copper azole (CA-B). These alternatives were developed to address environmental concerns and provide safer options for wood treatment.</p></p>
