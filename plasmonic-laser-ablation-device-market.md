<p><strong>The Plasmonic Laser Ablation Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Plasmonic Laser Ablation Device market can be classified into three main types: YAG Laser, CO2 Laser, and Others. YAG Laser devices utilize a solid-state laser medium composed of yttrium-aluminum-garnet crystals to generate high-energy beams, making them suitable for precise and efficient material removal. CO2 Laser devices employ carbon dioxide gas as the lasing medium, creating infrared beams that are highly absorbed by organic materials. The 'Others' category encompasses various laser types like excimer, fiber, or diode lasers, each having its own unique characteristics and applications in the field of laser ablation.</p></p>
