<p><p>The polyhedral oligomeric silsesquioxane (POSS) market consists of various types such as POSS molecules, POSS molecular silicas, POSS chemicals, POSS monomers, and POSS silanols. POSS molecules refer to the discrete cage-like structures composed of silicon and oxygen atoms. POSS molecular silicas are similar to POSS molecules but also contain silica groups. POSS chemicals are the general term for various chemical compounds derived from POSS. POSS monomers are the individual building blocks used to construct larger POSS structures. POSS silanols are specific POSS compounds that contain hydroxyl functional groups attached to silicon atoms.</p></p>
