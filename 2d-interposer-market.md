<p><ul><li>Silicon</li><li>Organic and Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 2D interposer market can be categorized into three types: silicon, organic, and glass. Silicon interposers are widely used due to their excellent electrical properties, high thermal conductivity, and compatibility with conventional semiconductor processes. Organic interposers, made of organic materials, offer cost-effective solutions and design flexibility but suffer from lower thermal conductivity. Glass interposers, on the other hand, provide high electrical performance, exceptional thermal properties, and outstanding signal integrity. Each type caters to different requirements and applications, providing a range of options for efficient integration and stacking of various electronic components.</p></p>
