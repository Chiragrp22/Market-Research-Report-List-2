The Double U Type contains two U-shaped tubes, offering higher brightness and commonly used in larger areas like factories. The 2D Type refers to square-shaped lamps, suitable for general lighting in various applications. Lastly, the H Type consists of a straight tube with two parallel tubes, often utilized in retail spaces or offices for better illumination.</p></p>
