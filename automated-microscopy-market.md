<p><p>The automated microscopy market comprises various types of microscopes utilized for scientific research and analysis. Inverted microscope is commonly used for observing cells from the bottom, ideal for cell culture experiments. Fluorescence microscope detects fluorescent signals within samples, enabling visualization of specific molecules. Electron microscope provides high-resolution imaging using electron beams, suitable for studying nanoparticles and analyzing material structures. Scanning probe microscope examines material surfaces with high precision by scanning a probe over the sample. Optical microscope uses visible light to magnify and observe samples. Other types include confocal microscope, phase contrast microscope, and digital microscope, among others.</p></p>
