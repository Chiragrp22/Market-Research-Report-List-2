<p><p>Automatic car wash machines are divided into two main market types: gantry car wash and tunnel car wash. Gantry car wash involves a stationary structure with a large robotic arm that moves over the vehicle to perform the washing process. It is mainly suitable for individual car owners due to its compact size. On the other hand, tunnel car wash is a long corridor where cars move through while being washed by various automated systems. It is more commonly used by professional car wash businesses due to its high capacity and efficiency.</p></p>
