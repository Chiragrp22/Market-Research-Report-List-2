<p><p>Plate rolls, also known as plate bending machines or sheet rolling machines, are specialized equipment used to shape metal sheets into cylinders, cones, or other curved shapes. They consist of three rolls – one top roll and two bottom rolls – that exert pressure on the metal plate to deform it according to the desired shape. This process is commonly used in various industries such as construction, automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing to fabricate cylindrical or conical components like pipes, tanks, and pressure vessels.</p><p>The plate rolls market has seen steady growth in recent years due to the increasing demand for curved metal components in various industrial applications. <p><p>Plate rolls refer to machines used for bending and shaping metal plates. There are two main types of plate rolls based on their power source: mechanical and hydraulic. Mechanical plate rolls are operated manually or by motor, using gears and chains to form metal plates. On the other hand, hydraulic plate rolls utilize hydraulic pressure to bend plates, making them more efficient and suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types find extensive utility in various industries like construction, manufacturing, and automotive for fabricating cylindrical or conical shapes from metal plates.</p></p>
