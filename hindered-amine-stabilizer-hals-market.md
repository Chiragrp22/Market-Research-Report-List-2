<p><p>Hindered Amine Stabilizers (HALS) are chemical compounds used in polymers to enhance their resistance to degradation caused by exposure to UV radiation, heat, and other environmental factors. <p><p>Hindered Amine Stabilizer (HALS) is a type of additive used in the polymer industry to improve the durability and performance of various materials, including plastics and coatings. There are three main types of HALS on the market: polymeric HALS, monomeric HALS, and oligomeric HALS. Polymeric HALS are made up of multiple repeating units and offer long-lasting protection. Monomeric HALS consist of single molecules that provide good initial stabilization. Oligomeric HALS are intermediate in size and offer a balance between long-term durability and initial stabilization.</p></p>
