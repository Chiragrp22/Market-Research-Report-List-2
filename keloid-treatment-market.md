<p><p>The keloid treatment market encompasses various types of treatments aimed at reducing or eliminating keloid scars. Occlusive dressing involves placing a dressing over the scar to create a moist environment. Compression therapy involves applying pressure to the scar to flatten it. Cryosurgery utilizes extreme cold to destroy the scar tissue. Excision involves surgically removing the scar. Radiation therapy uses targeted radiation to reduce scar growth. Laser therapy involves using lasers to break down scar tissue. Interferon therapy utilizes injections of interferon to reduce scar growth. Intralesional corticosteroid injection involves injecting steroids directly into the scar. Other treatment options include silicone sheets, herbal remedies, and pulsed dye laser therapy.</p></p>
