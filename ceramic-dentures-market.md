<p><p>Ceramic dentures refer to a type of dental prosthetics made from ceramic materials. They are of two main types: removable dentures and fixed dentures. Removable dentures can be taken out by the wearer for cleaning, while fixed dentures are permanently attached to the mouth using dental implants or natural teeth as support. Both types offer aesthetic appeal, durability, and improved functionality. However, removable dentures provide flexibility in terms of removal, whereas fixed dentures offer a more stable and secure fit.</p></p>
