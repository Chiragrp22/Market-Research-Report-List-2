<p><p>The Skin Cancer Diagnostics market encompasses various types of skin cancers, including Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC), Squamous Cell Carcinoma (SCC), Malignant Melanoma, and others. BCC is the most common type, usually appearing on sun-exposed skin and rarely spreading to other parts of the body. SCC is the second most common, often arising from precancerous skin lesions and potentially metastasizing. Malignant Melanoma is the most aggressive type, originating from melanocytes and susceptible to spreading rapidly.
