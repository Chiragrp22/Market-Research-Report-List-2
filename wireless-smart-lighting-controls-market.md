<p><p>The wireless smart lighting controls market consists of different types of technologies, including Zigbee, Wifi, Bluetooth, Z-Wave, and EnOcean. Zigbee is a low-cost, low-power wireless standard, while Wifi enables wireless communication using local area networks. Bluetooth also uses short-range wireless technology for connectivity. Z-Wave is a wireless communication protocol designed specifically for home automation devices. Lastly, EnOcean utilizes energy harvesting technology, eliminating the need for batteries. Each technology provides wireless capabilities to control and manage smart lighting systems, offering various benefits and features to users.</p></p>
