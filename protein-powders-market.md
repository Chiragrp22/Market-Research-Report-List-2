<p><p>Protein powders can be classified into two main types: animal source proteins and vegetable source proteins. Animal source proteins are derived from animal products, such as whey protein from milk or collagen protein from bone broth. These powders are popular among athletes and bodybuilders due to their high biological value and complete amino acid profile. On the other hand, vegetable source proteins are obtained from plants, like pea protein or hemp protein. These powders are preferred by vegetarians, vegans, or individuals with dairy allergies, offering a plant-based alternative for protein supplementation.</p></p>
