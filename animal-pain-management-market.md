<p><strong>The Animal Pain Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Use</li><li>Internal Use</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Animal pain management market types can be divided into external use and internal use. External use refers to the application of pain-relieving substances on the outer surface of the animal's body, such as sprays or creams. These are absorbed through the skin to provide relief from pain and inflammation. On the other hand, internal use involves the administration of pain medications through oral, injectable, or intravenous routes. These medications work internally to target the source of pain and provide relief to animals suffering from various conditions. Both external and internal use play crucial roles in managing animal pain effectively.</p></p>
