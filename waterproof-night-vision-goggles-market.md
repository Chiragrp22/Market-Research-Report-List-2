<p><strong>The Waterproof Night Vision Goggles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterproof night vision goggles are devices that allow users to see in low-light or dark conditions. There are two main types of market for these goggles: image intensifier and thermal image. </p><p>Image intensifier goggles work by capturing available light and amplifying it to enhance visibility. They are more affordable and widely used by civilians, law enforcement, and the military. </p><p>On the other hand, thermal image goggles detect the infrared radiation emitted by objects, creating an image based on the differences in temperature. They are typically more expensive and are often used by professionals in specialized fields, such as search and rescue operations or wildlife observation. Both types of goggles provide enhanced visibility and are available in waterproof versions to withstand outdoor conditions.</p></p>
