<p><p>The box sealing machines market includes two main types: fully automatic carton sealers and semi-automatic carton sealers. Fully automatic carton sealers are equipped with advanced technology and can seal boxes without any manual intervention. They are suitable for high-volume production environments. On the other hand, semi-automatic carton sealers require some manual input and are more suitable for low-to-medium volume production settings. Both types of machines are used for sealing cartons efficiently and securely, but they differ in terms of automation level and production capacity.</p></p>
