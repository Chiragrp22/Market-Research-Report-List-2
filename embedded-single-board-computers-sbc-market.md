<p><p>Embedded Single Board Computers (SBCs) are compact computing devices used in various industries. The market types for SBCs include CPCI, VME, VPX, ATCA, and others. CPCI (Compact Peripheral Component Interconnect) SBCs are ideal for applications requiring high-speed data transfer. VME (Versa Module Eurocard) SBCs offer robustness and reliability for military and aerospace applications. VPX (VITA 46) SBCs provide high-performance computing and low-latency communication for defense and telecommunications sectors. ATCA (Advanced Telecom Computing Architecture) SBCs are commonly used in telecom infrastructure. Other market types encompass specific SBCs used in industries like industrial automation, healthcare, and transportation.</p></p>
