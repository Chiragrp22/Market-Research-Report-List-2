<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Polishing Pad Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard CMP Pads</li><li>Soft CMP Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor wafer polishing pad market comprises two major types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are made of rigid materials, such as thermosetting polymer composites, that provide high durability and resistance to wear. On the other hand, soft CMP pads are constructed with elastic materials, such as thermoplastic elastomers, which offer enhanced flexibility and conformity to the wafer surface. Both types of pads are used for chemical mechanical planarization (CMP) processes in semiconductor manufacturing to provide precise and efficient polishing of wafers for improved performance and yield.</p></p>
