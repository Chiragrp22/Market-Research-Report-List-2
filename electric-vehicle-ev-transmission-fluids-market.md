<p><p>Electric Vehicle (EV) Transmission Fluids Market refers to the industry that encompasses the production and distribution of fluids utilized in electric vehicle transmissions. These fluids can be broadly categorized into two types: Synthetic Oil and Mineral Oil. Synthetic Oil, composed of chemically engineered molecules, offers better performance and durability, with enhanced lubrication properties and resistance to extreme temperatures. On the other hand, Mineral Oil, derived from crude oil, is a more traditional option, providing satisfactory lubrication but lacking the advanced features of synthetic oil. Both types cater to different needs within the EV market.</p></p>
