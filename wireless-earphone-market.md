<p><p>The Wireless Earphone market consists of two main types - RF Wireless Earphones and IR Wireless Earphones. RF Wireless Earphones use radio frequency technology to transmit audio signals wirelessly. These earphones have a longer range and can be used without obstruction of sight. On the other hand, IR Wireless Earphones use infrared technology for signal transmission. These earphones require a clear line of sight between the transmitter and receiver and have a shorter range as compared to RF Wireless Earphones.</p></p>
