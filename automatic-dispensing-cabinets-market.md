<p><p>The Automatic Dispensing Cabinets market can be categorized into two types: Centralized Automatic Dispensing Cabinets and Decentralized Automatic Dispensing Cabinets. Centralized Automatic Dispensing Cabinets are large units centrally located in a healthcare facility, and they are controlled by a main server. These cabinets efficiently store and dispense medications or supplies to various departments within the facility. On the other hand, Decentralized Automatic Dispensing Cabinets are smaller units placed in specific departments or patient areas. They provide immediate access to medications or supplies, allowing healthcare professionals to efficiently manage patient care.</p></p>
