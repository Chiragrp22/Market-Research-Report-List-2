<p><strong>The Advanced Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Sided PCB</li><li>Double-Sided PCB</li><li>Multi-Layer PCB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The advanced PCB market encompasses three main types: Single-Sided PCB, Double-Sided PCB, and Multi-Layer PCB. Single-Sided PCBs consist of a single layer of conducting material, making them suitable for simpler electronic devices. Double-Sided PCBs contain conducting material on both sides, allowing for more complex circuitry and higher component density. Multi-Layer PCBs are constructed with multiple layers of conducting material separated by insulating layers, providing even greater design flexibility and allowing for more compact and advanced electronic devices. These three types cater to different complexity and functionality requirements in the PCB market.</p></p>
