<p><p>Flexible couplings are devices used to connect two shafts, allowing for the transmission of power or torque while compensating for misalignment. Double thread couplings have two sets of helical threads, ensuring a secure connection and improved load distribution. They are commonly used in heavy-duty applications. Parallel lines couplings are designed with two shafts aligned in parallel and joined by a flexible element. They are suitable for applications with small angular misalignment and transmit low to medium torque. Both types offer reliable and efficient power transmission.</p></p>
