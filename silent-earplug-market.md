<p><p>There are three types of silent earplug markets: Silica Gel, Low-pressure Foam Material, and High-elasticity Polyester Material. Silica Gel earplugs are made of a flexible gel-like substance that conforms to the shape of the ear, providing a tight seal against noise. Low-pressure Foam Material earplugs are made of soft foam that expands to fit the ear canal, offering a comfortable and effective noise-blocking solution. High-elasticity Polyester Material earplugs are made of a flexible and durable polyester material that provides both noise reduction and long-lasting use. These three types cater to different preferences and requirements of individuals seeking quiet and peaceful environments.</p></p>
