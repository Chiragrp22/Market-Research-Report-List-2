<p><strong>The Gate Driver Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Chip</li><li>Discrete Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gate driver chips are electronic devices that provide the necessary voltage and current signals to control the switching of power devices such as transistors and IGBTs. There are two main types of gate driver chips: on-chip and discrete module. On-chip gate driver chips are integrated onto the same semiconductor chip as the power device, offering compact size and low cost but limited flexibility. Discrete module gate driver chips are separate devices that are externally connected to the power device, providing higher flexibility and customization options but at a higher cost.</p></p>
