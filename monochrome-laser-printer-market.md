<p><p>The monochrome laser printer market includes two main types: single-function laser printers and multifunction laser printers. Single-function laser printers are designed solely for printing and offer high-quality black and white prints. They are typically preferred by users who only require basic printing capabilities. On the other hand, multifunction laser printers provide multiple functionalities such as printing, scanning, copying, and sometimes faxing, offering convenience and versatility to users with varying needs. They are commonly used in small offices or homes where space and budget constraints make it impractical to have separate devices for each function.</p></p>
