<p><p>A Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) Oxygen Generator is a device that uses the process of pressure swing adsorption to separate oxygen from other gases in the air. It works by using an adsorbent material, such as zeolite, to selectively adsorb nitrogen and other gases while allowing oxygen to pass through. This process enables the production of high-purity oxygen for various applications, including medical, industrial, and aerospace.</p><p>The market for PSA Oxygen Generators has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period.
