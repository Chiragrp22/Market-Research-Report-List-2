<p><p>Electrical power connectors can be categorized into two main types: DC connectors and AC connectors. DC connectors are designed specifically for direct current (DC) applications, enabling the flow of electrical power in only one direction. They are commonly used in devices such as batteries, solar panels, and electronics. On the other hand, AC connectors are designed for alternating current (AC) applications, allowing the flow of electrical power in both directions. They are widely used in residential and commercial buildings to connect various electrical appliances and equipment to the power grid.</p></p>
