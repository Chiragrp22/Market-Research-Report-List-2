<p><p>The stationary battery testing equipment market includes three types: cell testing, module testing, and pack testing. Cell testing focuses on analyzing and evaluating individual battery cells, assessing their performance, capacity, and health. Module testing involves testing battery cells that are combined into modules, ensuring their proper functioning and reliability. Pack testing encompasses the examination of complete battery packs, assessing their overall performance, efficiency, and safety. These testing methods are crucial for understanding battery behavior, improving their reliability, and maintaining optimal performance in various stationary applications.</p></p>
