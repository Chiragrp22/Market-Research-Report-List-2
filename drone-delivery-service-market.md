<p><p>Drone delivery services can be categorized into three market types based on the range they cover. Close range drones are designed to operate within a limited radius, typically within a few kilometers, making them suitable for delivering parcels within a small area or a neighborhood. Short range drones cover a larger distance, typically up to 50 kilometers, and can serve a more significant area, such as a city or suburban region. Mid-range drones can cover distances of up to 150 kilometers, enabling them to transport goods between cities or even across regions.</p></p>
