Audio frequency chokes are used to suppress noise and interference in audio circuits. Radio frequency chokes help filter out unwanted signals in radio frequency circuits. Each of these market segments caters to different applications and requirements for efficient noise suppression and signal filtering in various electronic devices.</p></p>
