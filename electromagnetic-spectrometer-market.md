<p><p>The electromagnetic spectrometer market is divided into two types: atomic spectrometers and molecular spectrometers. Atomic spectrometers are devices used to analyze the elemental composition of materials by measuring the absorption or emission of electromagnetic radiation by atoms. On the other hand, molecular spectrometers are used to study the structural and functional properties of molecules by measuring their interaction with electromagnetic radiation. Both types of spectrometers play crucial roles in scientific research, quality control, environmental analysis, and other industries.</p></p>
