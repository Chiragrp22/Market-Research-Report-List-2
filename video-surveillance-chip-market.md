<p><p>The Video Surveillance Chip Market consists of four main types: Front-end ISP Chips, Front-end IPC SoC Chips, Back-end DVR SoC Chips, and Back-end NVR SoC Chips. Front-end ISP Chips are used in security cameras to process raw image data and enhance image quality. Front-end IPC SoC Chips integrate image sensors and processors, enabling features like video compression and networking capabilities. Back-end DVR SoC Chips are designed for Digital Video Recorders, which record and store video footage. Back-end NVR SoC Chips are used in Network Video Recorders, allowing for centralized storage and management of video data over a network.</p></p>
