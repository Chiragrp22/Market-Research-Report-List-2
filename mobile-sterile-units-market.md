<p><p>The mobile sterile units market includes various types of sterilizers used to eliminate pathogens and maintain a sterile environment. Gaseous sterilizers employ ethylene oxide gas or hydrogen peroxide vapor to destroy bacteria and viruses. Ultraviolet sterilizers use UV light to disinfect surfaces, air, and water. Other market segments may include ozone sterilizers, dry heat sterilizers, or plasma sterilizers, which use different technologies to achieve sterilization.</p></p>
