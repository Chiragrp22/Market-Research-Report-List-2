<p><p>Coaxial isolators are devices used to protect sensitive electronic equipment from reflected signals and high power levels. They are commonly used in telecommunications, radar systems, and other high-frequency applications. These isolators work by allowing signals to pass through in one direction while blocking any signal reflections or power backflow. This helps to minimize signal loss and protect the equipment from damage.</p><p>The future outlook of the coaxial isolators market looks promising, with a projected CAGR of 4.20% during the forecasted period.
