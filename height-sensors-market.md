<p><p>Height sensors are devices used to measure the height or distance of objects or surfaces. There are two main types of height sensors: contact type and non-contact type. </p><p>Contact type sensors physically touch the object or surface to obtain measurements. They are commonly used in industrial applications where precise and accurate measurements are required. </p><p>On the other hand, non-contact type sensors use technologies like ultrasonic waves, lasers, or infrared to measure height without making physical contact. These sensors are often used in automated systems or environments where a non-intrusive measurement approach is necessary.</p></p>
