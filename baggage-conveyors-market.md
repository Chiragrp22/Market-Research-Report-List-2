<p><p>The Baggage Conveyors Market Types predominantly consist of three categories: Belt, Roller, and Destination-coded Vehicle (DCV) markets. In the Belt market, baggage is transported on a conveyer belt, making it suitable for handling various sizes and types of luggage. The Roller market employs rollers to move baggage effortlessly, ensuring smooth transportation. On the other hand, the DCV market utilizes vehicles with destination codes to transport baggage automatically to designated areas, increasing efficiency and reducing the margin for errors during transit. These market types cater to the specific needs and preferences of airports and other transportation facilities.</p></p>
