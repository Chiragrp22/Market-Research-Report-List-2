<p><p>The robot floor scrubbers market can be divided into two main types - riding and non-riding. Riding floor scrubbers are larger machines that can be operated by a person who sits on top and controls its movements. They are usually designed for cleaning large spaces and can cover larger areas in a shorter time. On the other hand, non-riding floor scrubbers are smaller and typically operated by remote control or automatically programmed to clean the floor without human intervention. They are more suitable for smaller spaces or areas with limited access.</p></p>
