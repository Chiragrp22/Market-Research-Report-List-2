<p><p>The Artificial Organ & Bionics Market consists of two major types: mechanical bionics and electronic bionics. On the other hand, electronic bionics involves the utilization of electronic systems and devices to enhance or replace human sensory or neural functions, for instance, cochlear implants that restore hearing or retinal implants that improve vision. Both mechanical and electronic bionics contribute to improving the quality of life for individuals with disabilities or diseases.</p></p>
