<p><p>An orbit motor, also known as an orbital motor, is a type of hydraulic motor that converts fluid pressure into rotational motion. It consists of a shaft and an outer race, which contains rotating pistons. As fluid enters the motor, the rotating pistons push against the shaft, converting the pressure into torque and rotational motion. These motors are commonly used in hydraulic systems for various applications, including agriculture, construction, mining, and industrial machinery.</p><p>The future outlook of the orbit motor market looks promising.
