<p><p>The blister machine market consists of three main types: flat-plate blister packing machines, roll-plate blister packaging machines, and roll blister packaging machines. Flat-plate blister packing machines utilize a flat plate to heat and form blister packs, while roll-plate blister packaging machines use a roll plate to perform the same function. On the other hand, roll blister packaging machines solely rely on rolls to heat, form, and seal blister packs. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of packaging manufacturers in the industry.</p></p>
