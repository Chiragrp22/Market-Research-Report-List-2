<p><p>The interior wall putty powder market consists of two main types: cement-based putty and gypsum-based putty. Cement-based putty is a mixture of cement, white marble powder, and special additives. It provides a smooth finish and is widely used for interior walls. On the other hand, gypsum-based putty is made of gypsum powder, white cement, and additives. It offers excellent adhesion and is commonly used for plastering walls before painting. Both types of putty are used to fill and smoothen imperfections on interior walls, enhancing their appearance.</p></p>
