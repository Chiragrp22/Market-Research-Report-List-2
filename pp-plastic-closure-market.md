<p><p>PP Plastic Closure Market Types refer to the different manufacturing techniques used to produce plastic closures made from polypropylene (PP) material. Compression Moulding is a process where heated plastic is placed in a closed mold cavity and pressure is applied to shape it. Injection Molding, on the other hand, involves injecting molten plastic into a mold under high pressure to create the desired shape. Both methods offer benefits like cost efficiency, high production volume, and versatility, making them widely utilized in the PP plastic closure market.</p></p>
