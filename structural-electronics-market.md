<p><p>Structural electronics is a revolutionary technology that combines electronic components and circuits with structures or surfaces to create smart and functional systems. It involves embedding electronic functionalities directly into structural parts, reducing the need for traditional rigid PCBs and enabling lightweight, flexible, and multifunctional devices.
