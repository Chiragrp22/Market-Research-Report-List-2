<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Planarization (CMP) slurries are abrasive fluids used in the semiconductor industry to polish and flatten surfaces during the manufacturing process. Prestonian Type slurries consist of a single abrasive particle size, providing a consistent removal rate, better control, and suitable for flatness requirements. Non-Prestonian Type slurries, in contrast, contain a mixture of abrasive particles with various sizes, allowing for more effective material removal on different surface topographies. Both types cater to specific polishing needs and can be chosen based on the desired outcome and surface characteristics.</p></p>
