<p><p>The cutter stapler market consists of two main types: Endo Staplers and Open Staplers. Endo Staplers are utilized for minimally invasive surgeries where small incisions are made, and the stapler is inserted through a tiny tube to reach the target area. Open Staplers are used in traditional open surgeries, where a large incision is made to access the surgical site directly. Both types play a crucial role in surgical procedures by enabling efficient and precise tissue cutting and stapling, promoting faster healing and reduced complications.</p></p>
