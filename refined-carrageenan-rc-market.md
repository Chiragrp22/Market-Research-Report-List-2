Kappa carrageenan is mainly used for gelling properties in food products like dairy desserts and meat products. Iota carrageenan, on the other hand, is primarily used for its thickening and stabilizing properties in dairy products and sauces. Lambda carrageenan is often used as a viscosity enhancer in dairy products, beverages, and processed meat products.
