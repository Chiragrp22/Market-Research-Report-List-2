<p><p>The portable gas detection market encompasses two main types of devices: wearable and non-wearable. Non-wearable gas detectors, on the other hand, are stationary devices that are placed at specific locations to monitor gas levels in a fixed area. These devices are larger and are usually mounted on walls or ceilings. Both types of devices are essential for ensuring safety in environments where exposure to hazardous gases is a concern.</p></p>
