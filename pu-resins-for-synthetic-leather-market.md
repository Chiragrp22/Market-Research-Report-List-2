<p><p>PU resins are extensively used in the production of synthetic leather, which is categorized into two different market types: dry-process synthetic leather and wet-process synthetic leather. Dry-process synthetic leather involves directly applying the PU resin on a fabric substrate with subsequent drying and pressing. In contrast, wet-process synthetic leather involves immersing the fabric in a bath of PU resin and then drying and pressing. Both types of synthetic leather manufacturing processes utilize PU resins to provide a durable and leather-like texture to the final product.</p></p>
