<p><p>Industrial control transformers are devices that provide electrical isolation and voltage conversion for control circuits used in industrial automation systems. They step down the line voltage to a lower level that is suitable for control devices such as relays, contactors, and solenoid valves. These transformers are designed to withstand the high inrush current associated with power-on switching events and provide a stable power supply to control devices.</p><p>The market for industrial control transformers is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing deployment of automation systems in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, energy, and chemical is driving the demand for industrial control transformers. Additionally, the growing need for efficient control and monitoring systems to enhance productivity and reduce operational costs further fuels market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the rising adoption of renewable energy sources and the integration of smart grid technologies are also contributing to market expansion.
