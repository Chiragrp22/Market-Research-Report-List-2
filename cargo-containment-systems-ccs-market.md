<p><p>Cargo Containment Systems (CCS) are used in the transportation industry to ensure the safe storage and transportation of goods, especially liquefied gases. There are two common types of CCS in the market: membrane type and independent type. </p><p>Membrane type CCS comprises a flexible membrane that acts as a barrier between the cargo and the outer environment. It is known for its lightweight and high durability, making it suitable for transporting a wide range of gases.</p><p>On the other hand, independent type CCS consists of a rigid containment system that is separate from the ship's structure. This type offers enhanced safety and containment features, ensuring the cargo remains secure during transportation.</p><p>Both types play a crucial role in the safe handling and transportation of hazardous or sensitive cargoes, offering different benefits depending on the specific requirements of the shipment.</p></p>
