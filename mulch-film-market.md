<p><p>Mulch film is a protective plastic covering placed over soil to enhance crop growth. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) are the three main plastic materials used for making mulch films. LLDPE films are strong, flexible, and have excellent tear resistance. LDPE films offer good moisture retention and flexibility. HDPE films are rigid, durable, and have high strength. Each type of film has its advantages and is used based on specific agricultural needs, such as climate, crop type, and soil conditions.</p></p>
