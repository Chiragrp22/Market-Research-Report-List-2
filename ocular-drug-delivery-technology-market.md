<p><p>Ocular drug delivery technology refers to the methods by which medications are administered to the eye for the treatment of various eye disorders. There are several types of ocular drug delivery technologies. Topical delivery involves applying medications directly onto the eye's surface. Ocular inserts are small devices that are placed in the eye to release medication slowly over time. Iontophoresis uses an electric current to deliver drugs into the eye. Intraocular implants are small devices that are surgically inserted into the eye to deliver medication over an extended period.</p></p>
