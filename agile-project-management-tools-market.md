<p><p>Agile project management tools are categorized into two market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. Cloud-Based tools are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing users to access them from anywhere with an internet connection. This provides flexibility, scalability, and ease of collaboration. On-Premise tools, on the other hand, are installed and run on local servers and accessed within the organization's network. They offer more control over data security and customization but require higher initial investments and maintenance efforts. Both types offer various features to assist in managing agile projects efficiently.</p></p>
