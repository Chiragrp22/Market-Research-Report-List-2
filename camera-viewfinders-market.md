<p><strong>The Camera Viewfinders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Viewfinders</li><li>Electronic Viewfinders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Camera Viewfinders are an essential part of a camera that helps photographers frame their shots. There are two main types of Camera Viewfinders: Optical Viewfinders and Electronic Viewfinders. Optical Viewfinders use a system of mirrors and prisms to reflect the image directly from the lens to the user's eye. These viewfinders provide a clear and natural view, but they do not offer additional information or overlays. On the other hand, Electronic Viewfinders are digital screens that display a live image captured by the camera sensor. They offer real-time exposure and focus preview, as well as overlays for additional information like histograms and shooting settings.</p></p>
