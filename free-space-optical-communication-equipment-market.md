<p><p>Free Space Optical Communication Equipment, also known as Free Space Optics (FSO), is a communication technology that uses light beams to transmit information through free space instead of using traditional cables or wires. This technology utilizes lasers or Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs) to encode data onto light waves, which are then transmitted through the atmosphere to a receiving point. FSO offers high-speed, secure, and reliable communication, making it suitable for various applications such as telecommunications, military and defense, healthcare, and transportation.</p><p>The future of the Free Space Optical Communication Equipment Market looks promising.
