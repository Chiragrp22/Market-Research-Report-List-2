<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) is a technology that converts optical fiber cables into continuous and real-time acoustic sensors. It can be applied in various market types such as DAS Interrogator (DASI) and DAS Processor (DASP). DASI is the hardware component responsible for converting the optical signal into digital data, enabling continuous monitoring of sound waves along the fiber. DASP, on the other hand, consists of software algorithms that process the collected data and analyze it for various applications like detecting leaks, monitoring perimeter security, or seismic activity.</p></p>
