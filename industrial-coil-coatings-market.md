<p><p>Industrial coil coatings refer to the protective coatings that are applied to coils made of metal before they are further processed in various industries. The market for industrial coil coatings can be categorized into three types - primer, back paint, and topcoat. A primer is the initial coating layer applied to enhance adhesion and prevent corrosion, while the back paint provides additional protection and improves aesthetic appearance. The topcoat is the final layer that delivers color, gloss, and durability to the coated coil, ensuring long-lasting performance.</p></p>
