<p><p>The Agricultural Animal Vaccine Market is divided into three main types: live attenuated vaccines, inactivated vaccines, and others. Inactivated vaccines are created using killed pathogens, stimulating a less potent immune response. The "others" category encompasses recombinant vaccines, DNA vaccines, and vector vaccines. These variations serve different purposes in safeguarding the health and wellbeing of agricultural animals, helping to prevent and control diseases within their populations.</p></p>
